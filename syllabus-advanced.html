Generative AI for Advanced

Designed for people who want to learn more advanced skills.

Course Overview

This advanced-level course is designed for individuals who already have basic knowledge of Generative AI tools and wish to explore more advanced techniques. Learn to customize AI tools, integrate them into workflows, and use them creatively and powerfully. Build real-world projects and gain insights into cutting-edge applications of AI.

Course Objectives

  • Understand advanced capabilities of Generative AI tools like ChatGPT, DALL-E, and others
  • Learn to fine-tune AI tools for specific tasks and industries
  • Integrate AI into workflows and applications
  • Explore creative and innovative use cases for AI
  • Build and present an advanced AI-driven project

Target Audience

  • Individuals with basic knowledge of AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, DALL-E)
  • Professionals seeking advanced skills for creative and business purposes
  • Content creators, developers, and innovators looking to deepen AI expertise

Course Duration

8 weeks (1 session per week, 2 hours per session)

Syllabus Outline

Week 1: Advanced Prompt Engineering

  • Recap of prompt fundamentals
  • Techniques for crafting multi-step and layered prompts
  • Advanced use cases: storytelling, data extraction, and decision-making
  • Hands-on Activity: Write complex prompts for specific creative or business needs

Week 2: Fine-Tuning ChatGPT for Custom Applications

  • Introduction to API integrations and custom workflows
  • Training ChatGPT for industry-specific tasks
  • Case studies: AI in legal, education, and creative industries
  • Hands-on Activity: Create a custom ChatGPT application using OpenAI API

Week 3: Advanced Image Creation with DALL-E

  • Techniques for achieving consistent and detailed image results
  • Combining text and visual elements for professional projects
  • Exploring image editing and refinement tools
  • Hands-on Activity: Design a complete image set for a brand or project

Week 4: Integrating AI with Other Tools

  • Overview of tools like Zapier, Notion, and Canva with AI
  • Building multi-tool workflows for productivity and creativity
  • Automating processes across platforms
  • Hands-on Activity: Create an AI-integrated workflow for a project

Week 5: Developing AI-Enhanced Content Strategies

  • Generative AI for advanced content creation: blogs, videos, and campaigns
  • Personalizing AI for target audiences and niches
  • Evaluating AI outputs for quality and effectiveness
  • Hands-on Activity: Develop an AI-driven content strategy for a business or portfolio

Week 6: Ethics, Privacy, and Advanced AI Governance

  • Navigating ethical concerns in advanced AI applications
  • Best practices for privacy and data security
  • Exploring legal considerations and compliance in AI usage
  • Hands-on Activity: Create guidelines for ethical AI use in a project

Week 7: Creative Innovations with AI

  • Exploring cutting-edge AI capabilities: music generation, 3D modeling, and beyond
  • Experimenting with unconventional uses of Generative AI
  • Brainstorming innovative solutions to real-world problems using AI
  • Hands-on Activity: Prototype an AI-driven creative solution

Week 8: Capstone Project Presentation

  • Developing a final project that integrates advanced AI skills
  • Presenting your project to the group
  • Peer feedback and discussion
  • Certification and wrap-up: Planning your future with Generative AI

Learning Resources

  • Access to OpenAI APIs (e.g., ChatGPT Pro, DALL-E)
  • Optional tools: Zapier, Figma, Canva Pro, and Notion
  • Advanced guides, templates, and project examples

Conclusion

This course takes your AI skills to the next level, enabling you to innovate, customize, and lead in the age of Generative AI. Dive deep and transform your ideas into reality!
