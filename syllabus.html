Generative AI for Beginners

Unlock the Potential of AI in Your Everyday Life

Course Overview

This beginner-friendly course introduces you to the exciting world of Generative AI. Learn how tools like ChatGPT, DALL-E, and others can help you create engaging content, automate tasks, and enhance your creativity. No prior experience is necessaryâ€”just your curiosity!

Course Objectives

  • Understand advanced capabilities of Generative AI tools like ChatGPT, DALL-E, and others
  • Learn to fine-tune AI tools for specific tasks and industries
  • Integrate AI into workflows and applications
  • Explore creative and innovative use cases for AI
  • Build and present an advanced AI-driven project

Target Audience

  • Beginners with no prior experience in AI
  • Individuals interested in enhancing creativity and productivity
  • Small business owners, content creators, and students curious about AI tools

Course Duration

6 weeks (1 session per week, 2 hours per session)

Syllabus Outline

Week 1: Introduction to Generative AI

  • What is AI? A brief overview
  • What is Generative AI? How does it work?
  • Introduction to tools: ChatGPT, DALL-E, and others
  • Exploring the potential of AI in everyday life
  • Hands-on Activity: Setting up and exploring ChatGPT

Week 2: Getting Creative with ChatGPT

  • Understanding ChatGPT: Capabilities and limitations
  • Writing prompts for effective AI responses
  • Using ChatGPT for writing emails, blogs, and social media posts
  • Brainstorming ideas and solving problems
  • Hands-on Activity: Create a short blog or post using ChatGPT

Week 3: Visual Creativity with DALL-E

  • Understanding AI-generated art and design
  • Introduction to DALL-E: Creating images from text prompts
  • Applications for content creation, branding, and presentations
  • Hands-on Activity: Generate a custom image for a project

Week 4: Automating Everyday Tasks with AI

  • Overview of AI tools for productivity
  • Streamlining workflows: Automating repetitive tasks
  • Hands-on Activity: Use ChatGPT to create templates

Week 5: Ethics and Best Practices in Generative AI

  • Ethical considerations in using AI
  • Understanding bias and responsible AI use
  • Discussing privacy and copyright issues
  • Hands-on Activity: Analyze and refine AI-generated outputs responsibly

Week 6: Real-World Applications and Future of AI

  • Exploring real-world examples of AI in different industries
  • Customizing AI tools for personal or professional projects
  • The future of Generative AI: Trends and innovations
  • Final Project: Create a complete AI-enhanced project
